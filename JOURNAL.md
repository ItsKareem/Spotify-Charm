**Spotify Player Schematic is DONE!**

I did a lot of research on this and similar projects and I finally finished the schematic, here's how.

First, I chose to use a lipo battery and I followed the instructions I found from multiple sources online and mainly the documentation.

Then I connected the battery stuff and the screen I wanted to the WEMOS ESP32-C3 mini and I did it all according to the documentation too (which was confusing because the screen and the esp32 used different names for the same things)

Then I connected the 3 buttons, power button, and rotary encoder for volume and THAT'S IT!
